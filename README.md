# 🌍 TripFlow AI

> **Plan Less. Experience More.**

TripFlow AI is an AI-powered travel copilot that automatically creates optimized travel itineraries based on accommodations, destinations, travel style, weather, opening hours, and real-time conditions.

Instead of spending hours switching between maps, blogs, hotel websites, weather apps, and restaurant reviews, travelers simply tell TripFlow where they are going.

TripFlow plans the rest.

---

## ✨ Vision

Travel planning should take minutes—not hours.

TripFlow helps travelers make better decisions before and during their trips by intelligently optimizing routes, schedules, and recommendations.

Our long-term goal is to build the world's smartest AI travel copilot.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### ✅ Smart Itinerary Generation

Generate optimized travel schedules automatically.

### ✅ Multiple Accommodation Support

Support trips with multiple hotels or accommodations.

### ✅ Route Optimization

Optimize routes based on

- Distance
- Travel Time
- Opening Hours
- Weather
- Waiting Time

### ✅ Travel Style

Personalize itineraries for

- Family
- Couple
- Solo
- Friends
- Parents

### 🚧 Future Features

- Flight booking
- Hotel booking
- Rental car booking
- Budget optimization
- AI recommendations
- Live trip assistant
- Offline travel mode
